Welcome to One Night Ultimate Werewolf!

One Night Ultimate Werewolf is a fast-paced game where everyone gets a role: One of the dastardly Werewolves, or one of the other villagers, each with a special ability.

In the course of a single morning, your village will decide who is a werewolf... because all it takes is lynching one werewolf to win!

Join a Game

How to Play

1. Night Phase: Players perform their night actions in order.

2. Day Phase: Players discuss and try to determine who the werewolves are.

3. Voting: All players vote for who they think is a werewolf.

4. Resolution: If a werewolf is killed, the villagers win. Otherwise, the werewolves win!
